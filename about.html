about

An Afro / Indo Caribbean composer and creative technologist born and raised in the global city of Miami, known for blending the sonic aesthetics of different genres of music with immersive experiences.

Emerging from Miami’s vibrant club circuit, he became a regular in the rotation at iconic Miami Beach venues like Mansion Nightclub and Louis Bar, rocked rooms from the US to Switzerland, been featured as a DJ to go see in Miami New Times, and his music has been featured on editorial and official playlists ranging from YouTube Music to Pandora, and beyond!

Drawing inspiration from multidisciplinary creators like Pharrell, Tycho, and Deadmau5, Paul has focused on building music that bridges live performance, original music, and the visual arts.

What’s next? Stay tuned for my next project, "The Endless Nights" EP, a journey told through melodies, dancing, and cocktails!
