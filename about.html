about

An Afro / Indo Caribbean composer and creative technologist born and raised in the global city of Miami,

Paul is known for bledning the sonic aesthetics of different genres of music with immersive visual experiences.

Emerging from Miami’s vibrant club circuit, he became a regular in the rotation at iconic Miami Beach venues like Mansion Nightclub and bars like the beloved Louis, has rocked rooms from the US to Switzerland, and his music has been featured on editorial and official playlists ranging from YouTube Music to Pandora.

Drawing inspiration from multidisciplinary creators like Pharrell, Tycho, and Deadmau5, Paul has focused on building music that bridges live performance, original music, and the visual arts.
