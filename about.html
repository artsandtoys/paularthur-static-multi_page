about

An indo / afro caribbean artist and composer born and raised in Miami-Dade county, Paul is known for his blending of different styles of music and instrumentation into sweaty experiences.

Emerging from Miami’s club circuit, he became a regular in the rotation at celebrated Miami venues like Bardot and Mansion Nightclub, to overseas hotspots like Le Ciel, and featured as a DJ to go see in publications like the New Times.

Drawing inspiration from multi-disciplinary creators like Pharrell, Tycho, and Deadmau5, Paul has focused on building music that bridges DJ sets with live instruments, original music, and immersive visuals.

His last project, "Endless Bloom", is featured on editorial playlists from YouTube Music to Pandora to SXM. Stay tuned for the "Endless Nights" EP, a journey told through cocktails, dance music, and nights out!!!
